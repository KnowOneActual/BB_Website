Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

The tl;dr (Too Long; Didn't Read)

I value your privacy. I don't sell your data, I don't use invasive trackers, and I only collect the minimum information necessary to make this site work for you (like when you use the contact form).

Information I Collect

Contact Form

When you use the contact form, I collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Server Logs

Standard server logs (like IP addresses and browser types) are collected by Netlify (my hosting provider) to help maintain security and performance.

Third-Party Services

I use a few trusted third-party services to enhance the site:

  • Netlify: For hosting and serverless functions.
  • Cloudflare: For DNS management and security (Turnstile bot protection).
  • Google Fonts: For typography.
  • OpenWeatherMap: Used by the weather bot (if you use it).

Your Rights

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal data I hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected or deleted. Just reach out via the contact form on the home page.
