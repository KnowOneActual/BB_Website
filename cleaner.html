Secure Image Cleaner

A privacy-focused tool to remove metadata from your images.

About This Tool

When you take a photo, your camera saves hidden information inside the image file, known as EXIF metadata. This can include your location (GPS coordinates), the make and model of your device, and even the date and time the photo was taken.

This tool removes that metadata, creating a clean version of your image that you can share more privately.
