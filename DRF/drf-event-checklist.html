Event Planning Checklist

A comprehensive checklist for pre‑event, during‑event, and post‑event tasks at the David Rubenstein Forum. Check off items as you complete them—your progress is saved automatically.

← Back to Resources
📋

Pre‑Event Preparation

Tasks to complete 1–4 weeks before the event

  • Verify with Rubenstein Forum event coordinator
  • Include presenter, slides, and any special requirements
  • Request at least 48 hours before event
  • Confirm dietary restrictions and delivery times
  • Include parking info, Wi‑Fi details, and contact person
  • Verify audio levels, camera angles, and network stability
  • Digital displays, printed signs, and room‑number markers
🎤

During the Event

Day‑of tasks and real‑time coordination

  • Confirm microphone, slide advance, and pointer
  • Begin 5 minutes before scheduled start time
  • Adjust as needed for comfort and visibility
  • Provide microphone to audience, moderate questions
  • Place on chairs or provide at registration
  • Refer to Extron Quick Start Guide if needed
  • Document key moments for promotion and archives
📁

Post‑Event Wrap‑Up

Tasks to complete within 1–7 days after the event

  • Upload to secure storage and create backup
  • Summarize key takeaways for improvement
  • Include highlights and any follow‑up actions
  • Catering, AV rentals, and other expenses
  • Attendance numbers, contact information, notes
  • Via email list or event page
  • Discuss successes, challenges, and lessons learned
