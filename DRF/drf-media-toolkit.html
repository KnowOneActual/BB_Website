        Troubleshooting library

        Interactive versions of the operational issues that matter most in boardrooms and event spaces.

        Symptom Likely root cause Recommended first actions
        No signal or blurry output Bad handshake, wrong laptop resolution, unstable EDID Force 1080p60, set switcher EDID to static, re-handshake the input path.
        Desktop visible but streaming video black HDCP path issue or encrypted playback over unsupported chain Disable HDCP where appropriate, simplify the display path, avoid unnecessary inline capture devices.
        Audio dies after a few minutes IGMP snooping with no querier, or power-saving features hurting timing Enable IGMP querier, disable EEE, verify multicast is intentionally handled.
        AVoIP freezes or packet corruption Insufficient uplink bandwidth, MTU mismatch, or packet fragmentation Confirm 10Gb uplinks where needed, align jumbo frame settings, verify port membership and QoS policy.

        This section is intentionally concise here because the app is meant to turn these into faster operator workflows, not just static notes.
